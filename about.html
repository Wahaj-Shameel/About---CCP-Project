who we are

we are a premium
sofa manufacturing
& design studio

At Shameel & Co., we believe that a sofa is the heart of every home. Founded with a passion for exceptional craftsmanship, we have dedicated ourselves to creating seating solutions that bridge the gap between luxury and lasting durability. Our team of expert artisans combines traditional techniques with modern ergonomics to ensure every piece is a masterpiece.

From selecting the finest seasoned hardwoods for our frames to sourcing premium upholstery fabrics and high-resilience foams, we never compromise on quality. Our mission is to transform your living space into a sanctuary of comfort.

what we do

we craft bespoke &
ergonomic seating

We specialize in the end-to-end creation of premium sofa sets, sectionals, and executive office seating. Our design process is collaborative; we listen to your needs for space and style to produce furniture that fits your life perfectly. Whether it is a minimalist modern lounge or a grand classic set, we bring your vision to life.

Our commitment extends beyond delivery. We ensure that every stitch and every spring is placed with precision, providing you with furniture that maintains its shape and comfort for years to come. Experience the difference of artisanal furniture built specifically for you.
